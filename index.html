Maxime du confiné By Julien Neiertz

Once upon a time...

I took a break...

My story is simple. I couldn't go in the outside world as there was a terrible virus going around. Just like the one in the movies. I was stuck. At home. But .. before the Governement ask us to stay home, I made a wise choice. I went to the mountains. Then I was stuck in the mountains. In a quaint little chalet in a small village. Best choice I made, in a long time.

And I created this...

So here I was, all by myself, with something I didn't have in a long time. TIME. Yes, I finally I had the time to do those things I always wanted to do. Where to start? I'm blank. Maybe I will go for a nap first. Ah where was I? Right, make the most of this "Time"... Oh I know.. I love writing! And that's how my story begun. After that nap.
